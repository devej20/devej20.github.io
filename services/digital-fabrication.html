Digital Fabrication

Digital Fabrication

Some designs are simply challenging to develop with conventional fabrication techniques.

On the other hand, we do not want fabrication to pose undesired challenges to the design goals of clients.

At Cinctamore, enjoy research through making, hence, we continually seeking avenues that critically engage and explore the capacity of the tools at our disposal to create high quality and consistent products.

We are also conscious that, we serve some of the most rapidly and technologically evolving industries. These industries demand the cutting-edge technologies and solutions.

That is why we have invested heavily in state-of-the-art design and visualization software when it comes to our workflow.

Our Digital Fabrication Factories are stocked with CNC Machines readily available for prototyping and actual fabrication. Our ability to generate prototypes (models) swiftly makes our workflow efficient and in the long terms saves us resources for our clients’ success.

  • Packaging
  • Leather Goods
  • Apparel
  • Technical Textiles
  • Composites
