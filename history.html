Church History

We are a community of believers who are committed to following the teachings of the Holy Bible, and expanding God's family together through Jesus Christ. We strive to pass down our faith to future generations through Salvation and Repentance, guided by the Trinity.

As followers of the Holy Bible, we believe in the Trinity and the redemptive power of Jesus Christ, seeking salvation and repentance. We are committed to expanding God's family together for generations to come.
